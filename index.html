The Mission

Preserving the Puadhi Dialect in our Podcast.

The Puadh Punjabi Podcast is the first digital platform dedicated to the culture, history, and humor of the Puadh region.

While the world knows Majha and Malwa, our stories often go unheard. We are here to change that with unfiltered conversations, raw comedy, and deep dives into our village roots.

Puadh Punjabi Podcast is hosted by Gurinder Singh Dhanoa from Chandigarh, Punjab. The show covers the Puadh region — a historic area of Punjab including Mohali, Ropar, Patiala, and Ambala — through unscripted conversations about culture, NRI life, IT careers, and desi comedy. With 29+ episodes and over 1 million total views, it is the first podcast dedicated to preserving the Puadhi dialect.
