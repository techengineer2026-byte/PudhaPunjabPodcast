Sat Sri Akal! I am Gurinder Singh Dhanoa.

I started this podcast with a simple mission: to represent the Puadh region on the global stage.

While everyone talks about Majha and Malwa, our dialect and stories often get left behind. I'm here to change that. Whether it's discussing village history, modern struggles, or just sharing a laugh—this is the home of unfiltered Punjabi conversation.

I am from Mohali, 2024 में ਮੈਂ ਇਹ podcast ਸ਼ੁਰੂ ਕੀਤੀ when I realised no digital platform was documenting Puadhi culture. Outside the podcast, I work in IT developer and Social Media — which gives me access to some of the most interesting stories from our region.
