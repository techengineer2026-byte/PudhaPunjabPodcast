Chandigarh's Premier Punjabi Podcast

Puadh Punjabi Podcast records from its studio at Phase 8B, Sector 74, Mohali — just minutes from Chandigarh's city centre. Every episode features authentic conversations with entrepreneurs, IT professionals, NRIs, and cultural figures from the Chandigarh Tricity area and the wider Puadh region.

We celebrate the unique Puadhi dialect — the regional variation of Punjabi spoken in Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, Ropar, Patiala, and Ambala — and preserve its culture through unscripted, candid conversations that you won't find anywhere else.

With 29+ episodes and over 1 million total views, Puadh Punjabi Podcast is the Tricity's most-watched Punjabi podcast. Guests from Chandigarh, Panchkula, Kharar, Banur, Rajpura, and across the Puadh region visit the Mohali studio for in-person recordings — or join remotely from across India and abroad.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a podcast based in Chandigarh?

Yes — Puadh Punjabi Podcast records from its studio at Plot F-298, R&R Tower, Phase 8B, Sector 74, Mohali — in the heart of the Chandigarh Tricity area. The show covers entrepreneur stories, Puadhi culture, NRI life, and local history from Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, and the wider Puadh region.

What topics does the Chandigarh podcast cover?

The podcast covers IT careers, NRI lifestyle, Punjabi entrepreneur stories, farming, desi comedy, and the culture and history of the Puadh region including Chandigarh, Mohali, Ropar, Patiala, and Ambala. All conversations are in the authentic Puadhi dialect.

Where is the Puadh Punjabi Podcast studio located?

The studio is at Plot F-298, R&R Tower, Phase 8B, Industrial Area, Sector 74, Mohali, Punjab 160055 — easily accessible from Chandigarh, Panchkula, and Kharar. Contact us on WhatsApp at +91 83607 69451 to book a recording slot.
