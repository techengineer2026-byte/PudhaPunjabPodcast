Chandigarh's Premier Podcast

Puadh Punjabi Podcast is proudly based and broadcast from Chandigarh, the capital of Punjab. Every episode features authentic conversations with entrepreneurs, business leaders, and innovators from the Puadh region.

We celebrate the unique Puadhi dialect and preserve the culture of this historic region through unscripted, candid conversations.

Whether you're an aspiring entrepreneur, business enthusiast, or someone interested in Punjab's culture and history, Puadh Punjabi Podcast is your go-to source for authentic stories.

Our recording studio is located at Phase 8B, Sector 74, Mohali — in the heart of the Chandigarh Tricity area. Guests from Chandigarh, Panchkula, Kharar, Banur, Rajpura, and across the Puadh region visit us for in-person recordings. With 29+ episodes and over 1 million total views, Puadh Punjabi Podcast is the Tricity's most-watched Punjabi podcast.
