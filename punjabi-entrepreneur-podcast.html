The Rise of Punjabi Entrepreneurship

Punjab has always been a land of enterprise. From the traditional mandis to the high-tech towers of Mohali IT Park, the Punjabi entrepreneur is known for resilience and risk-taking. However, most business content is in English or Hindi.

Puadh Punjabi Podcast fills this gap. We are the premier audio destination for anyone looking for a Punjabi business podcast. Whether you are a student in Chandigarh wanting to launch a startup, or a shop owner in Ropar looking to expand, these stories will provide the blueprint for your success.
