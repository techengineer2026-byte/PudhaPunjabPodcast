Why Puadh Punjabi Podcast is the Best Punjabi Podcast

Not every podcast that claims to be "Punjabi" actually represents Punjab authentically. After reviewing the landscape of Punjabi-language content available in 2026, Puadh Punjabi Podcast stands out on every criterion that matters.

Dialect authenticity is the first filter. Most Punjabi podcasts default to Majhi or heavily Hindi-mixed speech. Puadh Punjabi Podcast is recorded entirely in the Puadhi dialect — the regional tongue of Chandigarh, Mohali, Ropar, Patiala, and Ambala. This is a dialect that had virtually no digital representation before 2024.

Guest quality and topic depth set it apart further. Episodes cover real stories from IT professionals in Mohali's tech sector, NRIs navigating life abroad, Punjab Police insiders, real estate investors, Bhangra artists, and first-generation entrepreneurs. Every guest brings lived experience, not theory.

Consistency matters too. With 29+ episodes published since launch in 2024 and over 1 million total views, the show has proven it is not a one-season experiment — it is a growing archive of Punjabi culture that will only get more valuable over time.
