Chandigarh's Premier Podcast Studio

Puadh Punjabi Podcast operates from a professional recording studio located at Plot F-298, R&R Tower, Phase 8B, Sector 74, Mohali. We specialize in recording guests from the tricity region who are shaping the future of Punjab.

We celebrate the unique Puadhi dialect — the regional variation of Punjabi spoken in Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, Ropar, Patiala, and Ambala.

With 29+ episodes and over 1 million total views, we are the Tricity's most-watched Punjabi podcast.
