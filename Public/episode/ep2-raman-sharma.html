EPISODE #2

Golgappa Stalls Earn Lakhs" - The Dark Truth of Hotel Jobs & Business

Guest: Dr. Raman Sharma

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Dr. Raman Sharma exposes the unfiltered reality of the hospitality business. He discusses why many hotels fail despite heavy investment, the profitability of the food industry, and why Punjabi youth are eager to move abroad while ignoring high-paying opportunities in the local food sector. The episode serves as a complete guide for students aspiring to join Hotel Management and investors looking to open Hotels or Banquet Halls.
