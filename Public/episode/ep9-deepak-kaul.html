EPISODE #9

The King Maker: Deepak Kaul on the Science of Winning Elections & Punjab’s Political Pulse

Guest: Deepak Kaul

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Deepak Kaul, a veteran political strategist who has spent nearly two decades behind the scenes of major Indian elections. Deepak shares his unique journey from banking and equity analysis to the CM’s office, and how he has helped over 32 ministers navigate the complex world of politics. We dive deep into why regional dynamics matter more than generic ads, the shift from paper posters to mobile warfare, and the core issues like law and order and unemployment that are currently shaping the future of Punjab.
