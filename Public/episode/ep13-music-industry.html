EPISODE #13

The Reality of the Music Business: Independent Hustle, Label Politics & Rap Beefs

Guest: Karan , Navjot , Vahm

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Karan, Navjot, and Vahm — a trio of talented artists making their mark in the music world. Karan brings his expertise as a music producer, Navjot shares his insights as a lyrical wizard, and Vahm represents the new wave of rappers from Delhi. Together, they peel back the layers of the music industry, discussing the pros and cons of being an Independent Artist versus signing with a Music Label. We explore the gritty reality of the Rap scene, the necessity (or nuisance) of Controversy for fame, and how emerging technologies are reshaping the landscape for creators.
