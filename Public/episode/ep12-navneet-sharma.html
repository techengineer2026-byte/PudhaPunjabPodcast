EPISODE #12

Beyond the Degree: Naveen Sharma on the AI Revolution, Teaching Ethics, and Saving the Next Generation

Guest: Naveen Sharma

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Naveen Sharma, a veteran educator who transitioned from a PGT English teacher in prestigious boarding schools to running her own successful IELTS venture. Naveen shares her journey from an Army background to becoming an independent business owner. We explore the "conflict" between the current education system and industry needs, the hidden dangers of AI making students mentally weak, and the critical role of parents in preventing drug abuse and screen addiction in urban India.
