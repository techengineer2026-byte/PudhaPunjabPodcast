EPISODE #28

IT Professional to Airbnb Entrepreneur: The "Chubara" Business Model, Foreign Culture & Cyber Scams Exposed

Guest: Vikram

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Vikram who explains how to generate a second source of income by utilizing empty roof spaces to create "Chubaras" (Studios) and listing them on Airbnb. He breaks down the investment, ROI, and how he manages it alongside his IT job. The conversation shifts to his global experiences, comparing the work culture in the Netherlands and Japan versus India. Most importantly, Vikram exposes a dangerous Cyber Scam involving a fake "Life Certificate" update that drained a pensioner's account. He provides a step-by-step guide on how the scam works via APK files and how to freeze hacked accounts immediately using the 1930 Helpline.
