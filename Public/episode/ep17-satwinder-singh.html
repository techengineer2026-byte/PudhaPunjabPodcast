EPISODE #17

The Digital Shift: Satwinder Singh on Marketing Scams, AI, and the News Industry

Guest: Satwinder Singh

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Satwinder Singh, who shares his vast experience moving from the high-pressure world of news agencies and movie promotions to the evolving landscape of digital marketing. Satwinder provides a masterclass on platform-specific advertising (Meta vs. Google), warns about the mental health impacts of AI on the next generation, and gives crucial advice on how to protect your digital privacy and avoid common online scams.
