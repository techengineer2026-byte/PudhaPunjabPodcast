EPISODE #6

Beyond the Glamour: Jarman Sheikh on Sleeping in Parks, Stolen Credits, and the Reality of Music Labels

Guest: Jarman Sheikh

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Jarman Sheikh, a rising voice in the Punjabi music scene. Jarman opens up about his heart-wrenching struggle in Mohali, where false promises left him homeless and sleeping in parks. He sheds light on the "credit theft" culture in the industry, his deep respect for Parmish Verma, the impact of AI on vocals, and why he chooses to stay independent rather than signing restrictive label contracts.
