EPISODE #20

How to Join Punjab Police: SI Exam Strategy & Physical Test Guide

Guest: Prajwal Sharma & Davinder Singh Rathore

Punjab Police recruitment process, Sub Inspector exam preparation, Punjab Police physical test requirements, cyber crime investigation in Punjab, gangsters operating from abroad, and career opportunities in Indian police services are discussed in this podcast episode.

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Prajwal Sharma and Davinder Singh Rathore, two IT professionals who switched careers to join the Punjab Police. They share their experiences regarding the rigorous selection process, the differences between Central Paramilitary Forces (like CISF) and State Police, and the reality of weapon handling. We also discuss pressing issues like the rise of cybercrime, gangsters operating from abroad, and why youth should avoid illegal migration (Donkey routes) and focus on opportunities within India.
