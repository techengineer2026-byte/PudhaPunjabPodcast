Meet the Host of Puadh Punjabi Podcast

One Mic. Real Stories. Unfiltered Culture.

Sat Sri Akal! Welcome to the Puadh Punjabi Podcast — the voice of the Puadh region. Recording from our studio in Mohali, Punjab, right in the heart of the Puadh region near Chandigarh.

This podcast started in 2024 with a simple mission: to represent the Puadh region on the global stage. The Puadh region spans from the Satluj river to the Ghaggar, covering Mohali, Ropar, Patiala, and Ambala. While everyone talks about Majha and Malwa, our dialect and stories often get left behind. That's why this show exists.

Whether discussing village history from the Chandigarh belt, modern IT career struggles in Mohali's tech parks, NRI life stories, or just sharing a laugh in pure Puadhi — this is the home of unfiltered Punjabi conversation from Punjab, India.

Outside the podcast, the host works in IT and Social Media in Mohali — which gives access to some of the most interesting stories from the region.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the host of Puadh Punjabi Podcast?

The host is a passionate storyteller and IT professional from Mohali, Punjab. He launched the show in 2024 with a mission to preserve the Puadhi dialect and give voice to stories from the Puadh region — which includes Mohali, Ropar, Patiala, and Ambala.

Where is Puadh Punjabi Podcast recorded?

The podcast is recorded at the Puadh Podcast Studio at Plot F-298, R&R Tower, Phase 8B, Industrial Area, Sector 74, Mohali, Punjab 160055. The studio is easily accessible from Chandigarh and Kharar.

How many episodes does Puadh Punjabi Podcast have?

Puadh Punjabi Podcast has 29+ episodes covering IT careers, NRI lifestyle, Puadhi culture, desi comedy, farming, and local history from Punjab. The show has accumulated over 1 million total views across YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts.

What language is Puadh Punjabi Podcast in?

The podcast is primarily in the Puadhi dialect — a unique regional variation of Punjabi spoken in the Puadh region covering Chandigarh, Mohali, Ropar, Patiala, and Ambala. Unlike Majhi or Malwai Punjabi, Puadhi has distinct vocabulary and tone that the show works to preserve.

What is the mission of Puadh Punjabi Podcast?

The mission is to bring the Puadh region's culture, history, and language to a worldwide audience. While Majha and Malwa are well-represented in Punjabi media, the Puadh region's unique stories were going undocumented. This podcast is the first dedicated digital platform for Puadhi culture.
