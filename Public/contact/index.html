Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a guest on Puadh Punjabi Podcast?

Fill out the guest submission form above or the main contact form with "Guest Request" selected. You can also WhatsApp us directly at +91 83607 69451 or DM on Instagram @puadhpunjabipodcast. We're looking for stories related to Punjabi culture, IT careers, NRI life, farming, and entrepreneurship from the Puadh region.

Where is the Puadh Podcast recording studio?

Our podcast recording studio is at Plot F-298, R&R Tower, Phase 8B, Industrial Area, Sector 74, Mohali, Punjab 160055. It is easily accessible from Chandigarh, Panchkula, and Kharar — roughly 15 minutes from Chandigarh city centre. Free parking is available nearby.

Can I record a podcast episode at your Mohali studio?

Yes — our Mohali studio is open for guest recordings, independent podcast sessions, and collaborations. The studio is equipped with professional microphones, soundproofing, and video recording setup. Contact us via the form above or call +91 83607 69451 to book a slot.

Do you accept sponsorships?

Yes, we collaborate with brands relevant to our Punjabi audience. Select "Sponsorship" in the contact form or email us at puadhpunjabipodcast@gmail.com for rates and media kit.

How long does it take to get a reply?

We aim to respond within 24 hours on weekdays. For urgent matters, WhatsApp or call us directly at +91 83607 69451 during studio hours (Mon–Sat, 10 AM – 7 PM).
