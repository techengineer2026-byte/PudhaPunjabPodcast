Frequently Asked Questions

How much does podcast editing cost?

Podcast editing is ₹5,000 per episode. This includes editing up to 1 hour of footage, plus 6 Instagram Reels or YouTube Shorts. We use Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve for broadcast-quality output.

What cameras do you use for shooting?

We provide a wide range of cameras from Canon M3 to Sony FX3 and FX30. The camera is selected based on your project requirements. Shooting starts at ₹3,500, with pricing adjusted based on camera choice.

Can I rent just the studio space?

Yes! Studio space is ₹2,000 per hour. This includes the soundproofed recording room with podcast desk. Lights and additional equipment can be provided on request at extra cost.

How good is your podcast anchor?

Our anchor is smart, well-prepared, and knowledgeable across almost every topic. They always research before the podcast, ask strategic questions that help you get more reach, and make sure guests feel comfortable. Anchor service is ₹2,000 per episode.

Yes. Content is the most important part of any podcast. We prepare scripts tailored to your target audience and current trending topics so you reach the widest possible audience. Scripting costs ₹2,000 per episode.
