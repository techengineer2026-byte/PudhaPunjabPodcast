Frequently Asked Questions

How much does podcast video shooting cost?

Starting at ₹3,500 (originally ₹5,000) with Canon M3/M50 cameras. Pro shooting with Sony A7 series costs ₹5,000. Cinema-grade shooting with Sony FX3/FX30 costs ₹8,000. Price varies based on camera and equipment selection.

What cameras do you use?

We offer a wide range from Canon M3/M50 (entry-level, great for YouTube) to Sony A7III/A7IV (professional full-frame) to Sony FX3/FX30 (cinema-grade with S-Cinetone color science). The camera is selected based on your requirements and budget.

How many camera angles do you use?

Standard setup uses 2-3 angles — one wide shot of both host and guest, one close-up on the host, and one close-up on the guest. Premium shoots can add a 4th overhead or side angle.

Is lighting included?

Yes. Professional LED panel lighting is included in all shooting packages. We use adjustable color temperature lights with diffusion panels for flattering, even lighting on both host and guest.

What's the difference between Canon M3 and Sony FX3?

Canon M3/M50 produces clean 1080p/4K video — perfect for new podcasters on a budget. Sony FX3 is cinema-grade with S-Cinetone color science, 4K 120fps slow-motion, and a cinematic depth of field. The FX3 makes your podcast look like a Netflix interview.

Can you shoot at my location?

Yes! We offer on-location podcast shooting across the Tricity (Mohali, Chandigarh, Panchkula). An additional travel and setup fee may apply. Contact us for a custom quote.

Does shooting include editing?

Shooting and editing are separate services. Shooting starts at ₹3,500 and editing costs ₹5,000. We offer a Shoot + Edit combo at ₹8,500 and a complete package with all services at ₹12,500.

How do I book?

WhatsApp us at +91 83607 69451 with your preferred date and camera tier. We recommend booking 48 hours in advance for weekends. Or fill out the booking form above.
