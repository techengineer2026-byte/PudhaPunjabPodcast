EPISODE #27

Financial Traps, Skating & Wealth Building: A Candid Chat with Harsimran Singh

Guest: Harsimran Singh (Harsimmar)

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Harsimran Singh, a dynamic content creator known for his unique blend of skating and financial education. A former banker turned entrepreneur, Harsimran shares his insights on the dark side of the "easy money" mentality in crypto, the hidden traps of credit card debt, and the importance of financial discipline. We also dive into technical aspects of business structures, how the wealthy leverage debt against assets, and the potential impact of global market fluctuations on Indian investors.
