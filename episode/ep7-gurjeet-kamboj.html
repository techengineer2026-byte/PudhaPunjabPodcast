EPISODE #7

The IT Reality Check: Gurjit Kamboj on Training Scams, Practical Skills & Making Dollars in India

Guest: Gurjit Kamboj

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Gurjit Kamboj, a visionary leader in the Chandigarh-Mohali IT hub. From his humble beginnings in a small village to ranking 4th in Haryana's polytechnic exams and founding a multi-company tech group, Gurjit reveals the "Black Holes" of the IT training industry. He provides a roadmap for students to avoid "certificate-selling" scams and explains how to master skills that actually land jobs in the competitive global market.
