EPISODE #3

The IT Roadmap: Vikram on Project Management, South India’s Tech Hubs, and Breaking Career Myths

Guest: Vikram

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Vikram, a seasoned professional who has navigated the demanding landscapes of both the Telecom and IT industries. Starting from ground-level training in Chennai with Airtel and ZTE, Vikram climbed the corporate ladder to lead projects for major US firms. He shares his experiences of overcoming language barriers in South India, the critical role of communication skills in leadership, and a candid comparison of IT salaries vs. government jobs. Vikram also offers a powerful perspective on why the youth of Punjab should prioritize high-value skills over the risky path of illegal immigration.
