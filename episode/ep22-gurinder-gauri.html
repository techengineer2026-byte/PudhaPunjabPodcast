From Classroom to Crown: The Inspiring Journey of Gurinder Gauri | Mrs. Chandigarh & Social Reformer

Guest: Gurinder Gauri

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Gurinder Gauri, a powerhouse of talent who proves that age and marriage are no barriers to success. From her roots in Samana to winning the Mrs. Chandigarh title and pursuing a PhD while managing a family and a teaching career, Gurinder shares her insights on modern parenting, the changing education system, and her mission to uplift society through the Pukaar Foundation.
