About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Vinisha, a Science and Math teacher who has deeply explored the world of Spirituality. Vinisha bridges the gap between Quantum Physics and Bhagti, explaining how the Law of Attraction is more than just "wishful thinking." She dives deep into how our Subconscious Mind is programmed during childhood (0-7 years), the impact of parenting on a child's future, and how to use Healing techniques like the Ho'oponopono prayer to clear emotional blockages. Whether you want to achieve career goals or fix relationships, this episode offers a scientific approach to faith and manifestation.
