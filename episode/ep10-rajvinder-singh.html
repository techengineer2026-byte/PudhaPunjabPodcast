EPISODE #10

Beyond the City Beautiful: Rajwinder on the Hardships of Cab Driving & Chandigarh’s Nightlife Realities

Guest: Rajwinder

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Rajwinder, a resilient young man from Faridkot who moved to Chandigarh to build a life. Rajwinder pulls back the curtain on the "glamorous" life of the city, sharing his gritty transition from a corporate job to food delivery and eventually cab driving. From surviving a hit-and-run accident to witnessing the complexities of Chandigarh's club culture from the driver's seat, Rajwinder provides a raw look at what it really takes to earn a living on the road.
