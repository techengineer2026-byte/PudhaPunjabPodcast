EPISODE #18

Amazon, Flipkart & Quick Commerce: The Ultimate Guide to Selling Online

Guest: Akash Near

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Akash Near , Founder of Max Ecommerce Services. Akash shares his journey from a mechanical engineer to an e-commerce expert helping brands scale on platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit, and Zepto. We dive deep into how to select the right product, the potential of Quick Commerce, avoiding common mistakes new sellers make, and the reality behind "₹1 Crore Revenue" claims. Whether you are a student, a farmer looking to sell produce online, or a business owner wanting to go digital, this episode is your blueprint for success.
