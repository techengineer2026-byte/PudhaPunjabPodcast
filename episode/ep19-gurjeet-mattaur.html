EPISODE #19

Gurjit Mataur on Puadh Identity, Mohali Development & Punjabi Politics

Guest: Gurjit Mataur

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Gurjit Mataur , popularly known as the "Puadh Ka Chokra." Gurjit is a fierce advocate for the Puadhi dialect and culture. He gained fame for raising local issues in Mohali and Mataur, ranging from infrastructure to fighting against immigration fraud. Join us as he discusses why the Puadh region deserves more recognition in the Majha-Malwa-Doaba narrative and his vision for Punjab’s political future.
