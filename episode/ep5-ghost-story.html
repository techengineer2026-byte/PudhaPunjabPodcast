EPISODE #5

From Factory Floor to Viral Vlogs: Karan Sharma on the Hard Truths of Zomato Delivery & Social Media Conscience

Guest: Karan Sharma

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Karan Sharma, a prominent voice in the Tricity (Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula) vlogging scene. Karan gained massive popularity by documenting his life as a Zomato delivery partner. He shares a raw and honest account of his early struggles working in a factory, the physical toll of 14-hour delivery shifts, and why he chooses to stay away from lucrative but unethical betting advertisements. This episode is a must-watch for anyone looking to understand the reality behind the "flexibility" of the gig economy.
